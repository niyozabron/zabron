ABOUT US



Our History

Water Billing system was established in 2019 to support the rwandans to get quick online srvices. It serves all customers equally, regardless of the status, gender or economic sector.

Objectives and Values

  • To decrease the time taken to pay water bills
  • To encourage people to go with ICT services
  • To register new customers so that they can get online services
  • To listen to what people think about the services they get

Mission

Transformation of servises from older and slower way to new and quick way to get water billing services.

Vision

To have a big percentage of rwandans working with quick and automatic way of getting services
